About

Small companies, big opportunities. The Startup Career Fair is hosted by Spark SC, the student-led entrepreneurship initiative at the University of Southern California. The Startup Career Fair connects top university, student talent across the city with the best opportunities at high-growth startups in Los Angeles. Students will have the opportunity to engage in informative discussion with recruiters, apply for both full-time and internship positions, and learn more about the perks of working at a startup. The event is free for all college or university students. See you there!
