Startup Career Fair

in Los Angeles.February 19, 2015, E-Quad @ USC.10:00am · 3:00pm

About

The Startup Career Fair is hosted by Spark SC, the student-led entrepreneurship initiative at the University of Southern California. The Startup Career Fair connects top talent at USC with high-growth startups in the Greater Los Angeles Area. Students will have the opportunity to engage in informative discussion with recruiters, and learn more about the perks of working at a successful startup in LA.
