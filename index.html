Startup Career Fair

in Los Angeles.February 19, 2015, E-Quad @ USC.10:00am · 3:00pm

About

Organized by Spark SC and TechLA, the Startup Career Fair aims to introduce and connect the best and brightest of the USC student community to some of the forefront rising startups in the Los Angeles area. All attending companies will have a table manned by recruitment representatives. Students will be able to have intimate, informative discussions with company reps, and are encouraged to ask questions regarding the unique work environment and future plans of each startup.
